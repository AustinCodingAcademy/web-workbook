Featured Project

Professional Project

This project was from an actual client, that I worked on with my classmates, with our teacher overseeing. I experienced the project from conception to delivery.

The website was built on the client's need to have a form someone could enter the information into, and he would find the best service professional for their needs. When considering competition for this site, Angie's List came to mind in their same approach

The website includes six buttons that link to six different forms that can be filled out based on the customer's needs. The client specifically requested six different forms. My solution was that I created a div box to hold all of the forms with the overflow hidden, and only the one specific form would be shown when a button was clicked.
