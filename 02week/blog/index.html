The Life of Al

September 1, 2018

Aleister Growley was beamed down into an alley dumpster behind Guadalupe Street in Austin,Texas in August of 2011. He and brother Theo Ruffstable, began exploring an unfamiliar world. Not knowing the rules of the road, the two dummies set out to cross a crowded intersection. In a fortunate turn of events I happened to witness this foolishness, stopped the car, opened the door, the boys jumped in and the rest is history.
