California: Here I Come

Manifest destiny! I finally made it to San Francisco, where I spent the weekend with my brother taking in the Pier, Golden Gate Bridge, The Haight, and taking in a concert of our favorite shoegaze band, RIDE. It was a great trip for resetting my mental state as well as a great roadtrip with my older brother.

One of the highlights of the trip was the plane ride there. Watching the Rockies and the rest of the Western United States pass below me really put into perspective the trials and tribulations of my life at the time. Being unemployed with a newborn baby and the primary caregiver for my grandfather.
