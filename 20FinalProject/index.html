Why I chose to change careers

We all spend time on the internet and everyday more and more people are connecting their businesses and themselves to the digital world. No matter what you do for a living or how big your company is a presence on the web is imperative to your success. Creating a website that portrays your company accurately and is easy to find the information that is needed is important. This is your one employee that works 24-hours a day, 7-days a week.

I have been in this industry for over 25 years and I have seen great websites and I have seen sites that need improvement. My goal is to provide you and/or your company with the web presence that meets you where you are. I do not use “cookie cutter” designs and each design will be tailored to your needs and present the image you want to portray. A responsive and organized website is key to portray your image. I have seen beautiful websites that are impossible to navigate and this will only leave your clients frustrated and they will leave your site for a site that can be navigated easily.
